CryptoShocks

CryptoShocks is a research website on cryptocurrency-related news shocks and the equity-market exposure of listed firms to crypto developments. The material hosted here is generated from a structured pipeline that combines curated inputs, event charts, and large-language-model classification.

The website currently contains two public reports. The first classifies major crypto news events by whether they plausibly explain observed Bitcoin price moves. The second classifies crypto-exposed firms by their main transmission channel, such as holders, miners, financial services, infrastructure, or other business models.

Narrative Classification of Crypto News Events

Event-by-event report linking news headlines to Bitcoin price moves, with category labels, certainty scores, and event charts.

Narrative Classification of Crypto-Exposed Firms

Firm-level report assigning fractional weights across crypto exposure channels using transcripts, filings, and financial news.
