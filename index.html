Crypto Shocks

Tracing-how crypto news moves the market

Can fluctuations in cryptocurrency prices cause movements in traditional financial asset prices? The paper addresses this question by constructing a new series of exogenous crypto shocks: cryptocurrency-price changes that are unrelated to developments in the real economy or traditional financial markets.

The identification strategy combines narrative, high-frequency, and large-language-model approaches, and the empirical analysis traces the effects of those shocks on firm-level equity prices. To capture heterogeneous transmission, the project also builds a quarterly crypto-exposure measure for 1,234 publicly listed firms across more than 50 countries using text analysis of earnings call transcripts.
